# Evaluation Rubric

## Scoring Scale

1–2: Poor (does not meet expectations)  
3–4: Moderate (partially meets expectations)  
5–6: Good (mostly meets expectations)  
7: Excellent (fully meets expectations)

---

## Example-Based Rubric Illustration

To ensure clarity and consistency in evaluation, the following example demonstrates how captions are scored across the defined metrics.

### Sample Conversation (Transcript)

“I thought I’d be fine with it, but honestly it still stings a little. Maybe I just need more time.”

---

## 1. Relevance

**Definition:**  
Measures how well the caption reflects the main idea of the conversation.

- Score 1: “Living my best life every day ✨”  
  → Unrelated to the conversation  

- Score 4: “Sometimes things don’t go as planned.”  
  → Partially reflects the idea but lacks specificity  

- Score 7: “I thought I’d be okay with it, but it still hurts more than I expected.”  
  → Clearly reflects the main idea  

---

## 2. Emotion Alignment

**Definition:**  
Measures how well the caption matches the emotional tone of the conversation.

- Score 1: “Feeling amazing and grateful today!”  
  → Emotion is completely mismatched  

- Score 4: “Things have been a bit tough lately.”  
  → Partial emotional alignment  

- Score 7: “It still stings a little, even though I try to move on.”  
  → Strong emotional alignment  

---

## 3. Subtext

**Definition:**  
Measures how well the caption captures implied meaning beyond literal words.

- Score 1: “I talked about something that happened.”  
  → No implied meaning  

- Score 4: “Maybe I just need more time.”  
  → Some implied meaning  

- Score 7: “I keep telling myself I’m fine, but I’m not fully over it.”  
  → Strong subtext and nuance  

---

## 4. Authenticity

**Definition:**  
Measures how natural, human-like, and suitable the caption is for social media.

- Score 1: “This situation has contributed to my emotional processing.”  
  → Unnatural or robotic  

- Score 4: “Today was a bit difficult.”  
  → Acceptable but not engaging  

- Score 7: “Some things just take longer to heal than you expect.”  
  → Natural, relatable, and authentic
